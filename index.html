Personal Loan Singapore: The Ultimate 2026 Borrower's Guide

A personal loan in Singapore is an unsecured financial product that provides borrowers with a lump sum of cash for various needs—whether covering medical emergencies, funding home renovations, consolidating existing debts, or managing unexpected expenses. If you are looking for a reliable fast cash loan Singapore, licensed lenders offer a secure and regulated way to access funds quickly. Unlike secured loans that require collateral such as property or vehicles, personal loans rely primarily on your income stability and creditworthiness.

The entire licensed moneylending industry in Singapore operates under the strict oversight of the Moneylenders Act, administered by the Ministry of Law through the Registry of Moneylenders. This regulatory framework ensures that every transaction remains transparent, fair, and legally binding.

Eligibility Requirements for Borrowers

Understanding whether you qualify for a personal loan Singapore requires familiarity with the Ministry of Law's eligibility standards.

  • Minimum age: 21 years old
  • Stable income and employment
  • Valid identification (NRIC or work pass)
  • Proof of income (payslips or bank statements)

Interest Rates and Fees

Licensed lenders cannot charge more than 4% interest per month. Administrative fees are capped at 10% of the loan amount, ensuring borrower protection.
